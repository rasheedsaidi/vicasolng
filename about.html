About us
This is Vicasol Nigeria Limited

Introduction

Vicasol is wholly an indigenous company with a vision and passion, Vicasol was initially established in February,1994 as enterprise and later consolidated as Limited Liability Company (LLC) on the 11th February, 1997 since then the company from a small general services outfit into growing service provider with operational offices in Port Harcourt and Lagos while Business Development offices are in Lagos and Abuja respectively. We are small in size with lean overhead however with global experience and practise.

It worth mentioning that Vicasol experienced some setbacks for some period of time. However, in 2014 a new set of management team was put in place to take charge of the management of the company. They comprises of captains of the industries who had reputation in Oil and Gas in terms of project execution and service delivery. This development is behind this initiative of coming back and the advantage of huge opportunities in Nigerian Oil and Gas industries across the entire spectrum of Upstream, Mid-stream and Down stream.

Formation of Vicasol

Vicasol has been able to put in place a strategy of collaboration with Nigerian Fabrication and Installation companies in Oil and Gas after a careful due diligence, we have an MoA with Chief Engineer Nigeria Limited to use the Kris Fabrication Yard in Apapa as well as all resources upon contract award, this Fabrication yard can accommodate fabrication of Topside and any other fabrication work and it has a direct access to Water front making it accessible for Load out of Pre-fabricated work.

Furthermore, a similar MoA was put in place with CMES to engage there resources (Equipment and Manpower) as well as Yard at Naval Dockyard, along Ahmudu Bello Way, Victoria Island. These two facilities are currently available for fabrication and load out. See the attached MoA from these two organizations.

Broad Goals:

  • Grow the business without losing sight of government statutory & regulatory requirements
  • Build database and provison of support services to other stakeholders in Nigerian Oil & Gas industries
